About Maxivo Solutions

Many organizations find themselves dependent on a single vendor or agency for critical technology decisions—creating risk around ownership, transferability, and long-term control. Maxivo exists to address this. We provide independent, vendor-neutral guidance that helps you understand what you own, what risks exist in your current architecture, and how to evolve your platform without creating new dependencies. We work alongside your existing partners, not against them, to ensure your technology remains an asset you control.

Our Approach

We begin with assessment and advisory—not execution. Most engagements start with an independent architecture review or technology assessment, providing you with clarity and decision support before any commitments are made. Engineering work is only undertaken when you choose to proceed, and always remains optional. This approach reduces risk, preserves your flexibility, and ensures you maintain control over both the direction and pace of change.

Technology Expertise

Our team brings deep expertise across modern technology stacks including Java, Spring Boot, Node.js, Angular, Android, AWS, Azure, MySQL, MongoDB, CouchDB, and IoT systems with MQTT. We leverage this breadth to provide vendor-neutral, future-ready recommendations.

What we do

We provide independent technology advisory and selective engineering support to help organizations reduce ownership risk, understand their architectural position, and make informed decisions about platform evolution—without being forced into implementation commitments.

Independent Architecture Assessment

Objective evaluation of your current technology position: what you own, what risks exist, where dependencies lie, and what options are available. No sales agenda—just clarity on your architectural reality and pathways forward.

Vendor-Neutral Roadmap Planning

Technology evolution strategies that preserve your independence. Cloud migration, platform modernization, and architecture decisions designed to maximize portability, reduce lock-in risk, and maintain long-term control over your systems.

Selective Engineering Execution

When you choose to proceed beyond advisory, we offer targeted engineering support—backend, frontend, cloud, mobile (Android), and IoT systems. Engagements are milestone-based and structured to transfer knowledge, not create dependency.

Exit Readiness & Ownership Protection

Technology decisions optimized for your ability to change direction. We help ensure your platforms remain transferable, maintainable by others, and free from proprietary dependencies that limit your future options.

Low-Risk Entry Point

Start with a focused assessment or advisory engagement—no execution required, no long-term commitment. Gain clarity, evaluate your options, and decide if and when to proceed. You remain in control at every stage.

Respectful Collaboration

We work alongside your existing vendors and teams, not against them. Transparent communication about risks, trade-offs, and realistic outcomes—without undermining your current relationships or creating unnecessary friction.
